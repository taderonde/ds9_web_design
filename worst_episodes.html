Star Trek: Deep Space Nine

Lines by Character in the Worst Episodes

This plot shows the top 10 characters with the most lines in episodes with fewer than 2 stars (Jammer's reviews are out of 4 stars).

There are only 6 of these truly bad episodes, and unfortunate for Armin Shimerman (the actor who played Quark), he had a starring role in half of them.

3 of these episodes are Ferengi episodes, which generally range from bad to mediocre, so the other Ferengi (sans Nog) appear on the list.

Dax is surprisingly high, mostly for her role in that terrible Risa episode.
